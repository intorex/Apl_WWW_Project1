Lotte World Tower

Basic informations:

Height:  554,5m

Country:  South Korea

Description:  After 13 years of site preparation and planning, the Lotte World Tower gained final approval, and construction started in late 2010. Six years later, the tower was finalized and became South Korea’s tallest building, standing at 554.5 meters (1,819ft).

Lotte World Tower in Jamsil, Songpa-gu
 

Additional resources:

Lotte World Tower - Wikipedia

Lotte World Tower is a 555-meter supertall skyscraper in Seoul, South Korea, and the sixth-tallest building in the world. It has 123 floors, including an observation deck, a hotel, a mall, and a cultural center.
