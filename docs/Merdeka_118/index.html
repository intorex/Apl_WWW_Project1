Merdeka 118 - Wikipedia

Merdeka 118 is a 118-story megatall skyscraper in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, and the second-tallest building and structure in the world. It is funded by Permodalan Nasional Berhad, an investment management company owned by the Malaysian government, and will have office, hotel, retail, and observatory spaces.

Merdeka 118 Tower — Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Merdeka 118 is a 679-meter skyscraper in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, opened in 2024. It features a hotel, a mall, an observation deck, and offices.
