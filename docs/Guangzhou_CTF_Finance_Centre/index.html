Guangzhou CTF Finance Centre

Basic informations:

Height:  530m

Country:  China

Description:  The Guangzhou CTF Finance Centre is the third-tallest in China with a height of 530 meters (1,739ft). The tower is much more than just an office building. It hosts houses, a shopping mall, offices, apartments, and even a hotel.
